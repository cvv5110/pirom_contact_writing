\section{Introduction}


The objectives of this work as summarized as follows:
\begin{enumerate}
    \item Present the systematic application of PIROM to simultaneously learning a ROMs and inferring contact conductances for hypersonic TPS.
    \item Benchmark the PIROM performance against the state-of-the-art data-driven, SciML, and traditional FEM modeling approaches for inferring contact conductances, as well as for predicting the temperature dynamics of the system. 
    \item Assess the performance of the PIROM in an experimental setting, 
\end{enumerate}
